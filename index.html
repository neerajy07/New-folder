Our Process

How We Make Our Wormee Vemicompost

Step 1: Segregation, Homogenization and Sanitization

Quality cow dung is procured & homogenised, and stored until a temperature of 60℃, thereby sanitising the cow dung naturally.

Step 2: Raw Material Preparation

The raw material pile is cooled off by sprinkling water until a desirable temperature of less than 30℃ is achieved.

Step 3: Bed Making and Earthworm Broadcasting

The cow dung is then spread over the beds, earthworms are added and the beds are covered with jute bags.

Step 4: Harvesting and Packaging

Perfect conditions are maintained, until the vermicompost is ready. It is then harvested and packaged to retain moisture and freshness.
