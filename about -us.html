About Our Farm

We Care About Your Plants and Your Crops.

Wormee has a well-managed vermicompost farm located near Chandlai lake of Jaipur. We are dedicated to producing high-quality organic fertiliser using earthworms, and the wonderful people who work on the vermicompost farms. They are committed to creating a healthier environment by reducing waste and improving soil quality.

Going Back to Basics with Vermicompost

By choosing vermicompost over chemical fertilizers, you prioritize the long-term health and productivity of your plants and the environment. Vermicompost provides a balanced nutrient profile, ensuring that plants receive a comprehensive range of essential nutrients.
